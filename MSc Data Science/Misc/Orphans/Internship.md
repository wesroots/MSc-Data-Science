## âš™ï¸ Industrial Dissertation
**Definition:**  
Your MSc project uses a company dataset or business problem, supervised jointly by a Kent lecturer and a company contact.

**Key Points**
- You are not an employee; the work replaces your dissertation.  
- Company provides data or defined problem.

- Real-world project + company name on CV.  
- Stronger employability proof.  
- May lead to post-graduation job.  

**To set up**
